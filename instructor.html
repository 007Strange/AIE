About Mr. Salman Ahmed

Prof. Salman Ahmed is a well-known name in the engineering education circles of Hyderabad. He did his Diploma from The Government Polytechnic, Quli Qutub Shah, Hyderabad and went on to do his graduation from The College of Engineering, Hyderabad. He further pursued his Masters's Degree (M.E) from Osmania University.

Prof. Ahmed's journey in the teaching profession began in the year 2015 when he established Ahmed's Institute in Hyderabad. In a short span of time, the institute has become a well-known name in the city. He has motivated and guided thousands of engineering graduates for various competitive exams, and has been instrumental in producing top rankers.
